Fahd Ahmed

With over 10 years of experience in the culinary world, Fahd Ahmed brings passion, creativity, and dedication to the UH-Sugarland Cafeteria. Inspired by his love for cooking and serving delicious food, Fahd has created a dining experience that combines, comfort, quality, and affordability.

Fahd's journey in the food industry began at a young age, working alongside his parents in their family-owned restaurant. Here, he learned the importance of hospitality, attention to detail, and the joy of bringing people together through food. Throughout his career, Fahd has honed his skills in various culinary roles, from line cook to head chef. His commitment to excellence and innovation has earned him recognition among his peers and loyal customers.

Now as the proud owner of UH-Sugarland Cafeteria, Fahd is dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience for every customer who walks through the door. Whether it's a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a sweet treat, Fahd and his team strive to exceed expectations and create memorable moments for all.

Outside of the restaurant, Fahd is an avid supporter of local community initiatives, from organizing food drives to sponsoring youth sports teams. His commitment to giving back to the community mirrors his dedication to providing a welcoming and inclusive dining experience at UH-Sugarland Cafeteria.
